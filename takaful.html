Takaful Advisory

Shariah-compliant Takaful solutions designed to protect income, assets, and family legacy for High Net Worth Muslim families in Malaysia.

Service Overview

Our Takaful advisory helps clients identify risks, safeguard wealth, and ensure family security in accordance with Shariah principles. This service is tailored for individuals, business owners, and families who prioritize compliant risk management.

Who This Service Is For

  • High Net Worth Muslim families seeking Shariah-compliant risk protection
  • Business owners looking to protect income, assets, and family legacy
  • Individuals planning for unforeseen events and long-term financial security

What’s Included / How It Works

  • Comprehensive risk assessment of income, assets, and family obligations
  • Design of customized Shariah-compliant Takaful plans (life, medical, business, or family protection)
  • Periodic review and updates to ensure ongoing compliance and suitability
  • Guidance on claim process and beneficiary structure to preserve estate and legacy
