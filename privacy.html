Privacy Policy

Your data privacy is governed by the Malaysian Personal Data Protection Act 2010 (PDPA).

1. Collection of Personal Data

In the course of your dealings with HAS Wealth Advisory, we may collect personal data such as your name, contact details, NRIC number, and financial information. This data is collected when you fill out contact forms, subscribe to insights, or engage in advisory services.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

We process your personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide financial advisory services (Takaful, Unit Trust, Retirement Planning).
  • To comply with regulatory requirements from BNM, SC, and FIMM.
  • To communicate updates, newsletters, or appointment reminders.
  • To conduct "Know Your Customer" (KYC) and anti-money laundering (AMLA) checks.

3. Disclosure of Data

Your personal data is kept confidential. However, we may disclose your information to:

  • Phillip Wealth Planners Sdn Bhd and Phillip Mutual Berhad (as the principal companies).
  • Regulatory authorities, government agencies, or law enforcement as required by law.
  • Third-party service providers (e.g., IT hosting) who are contractually bound to protect your data.

4. Your Rights (Access & Correction)

Under the PDPA, you have the right to access your personal data and request corrections if the data is inaccurate, incomplete, or outdated. You may also withdraw your consent for us to process your data for marketing purposes.

5. Security of Data

We implement strict technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, accidental loss, or destruction.
