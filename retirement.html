Our Three Pillars of Retirement

  • The Accumulation Phase: Utilizing disciplined Unit Trust portfolios and Private Retirement Schemes (PRS) to build a robust foundation during your working years.
  • The Preservation Phase: Transitioning your assets into lower-volatility Shariah-compliant instruments as you approach retirement to protect your capital.
  • The Distribution Phase: Structuring a sustainable monthly "salary" from your investments to maintain your standard of living without exhausting your principal.

Why Structured Advisory?
